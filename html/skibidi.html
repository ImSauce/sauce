About This Game

This mobile visual novel game was developed strictly for educational purposes, aiming to provide an engaging platform for young learners to explore basic grammar, science, and mathematics concepts. The application was built using the Flutter framework, enabling cross-platform compatibility and smooth interactive experiences. This project was a collaborative effort with my colleague, Tony, during my first year of college. As a student-developed prototype, users may encounter occasional bugs, which are a natural part of the iterative learning and development process.

Target Users

The game is designed for children aged 9 to 14 who are developing foundational skills in English, mathematics, and science. The game combines educational content with interactive storytelling, ensuring that learning is engaging, enjoyable, and age-appropriate. Its visual novel structure and quiz-based mechanics are tailored to the cognitive abilities and interests of this age group.

Game Features:

- Character Illustrations
- Quiz Mechanics
- Story Mode
- Challenge Mode
- User-Friendly UI
- Health System

A mobile educational visual novel game designed to engage young learners in mastering fundamental grammar, science, and mathematics through interactive gameplay.
